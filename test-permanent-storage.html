💡 How Permanent is "Permanent"?

localStorage is truly permanent! Your token and user data will persist:

  • ✅ After page refreshes
  • ✅ After closing browser tabs
  • ✅ After closing the browser completely
  • ✅ After restarting your computer
  • ✅ After days, weeks, or months
  • ✅ Until you manually clear it or logout

🔒 When Does Data Get Cleared?

Your authentication data will only be cleared when:

  • ❌ User clicks the logout button
  • ❌ User manually clears browser data
  • ❌ User uses incognito/private mode (doesn't persist after closing)
